Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 4Spares.

How fast is delivery?

4Spares delivers spare parts in under 45 minutes. Once you place your order, our delivery network dispatches the part from the nearest verified store directly to your workshop.

Where does 4Spares currently operate?

We currently serve Kozhikode, Kerala. We're rapidly expanding to more cities across Kerala — stay tuned!

How do I order a spare part?

Download the 4Spares app on Android or iOS, or visit 4spares.in. Search for the part you need, compare prices from nearby verified stores, and place your order instantly.

What types of vehicles does 4Spares support?

We currently support heavy and commercial vehicles — trucks, buses, and other large commercial vehicles. We're continuously expanding our catalogue.

How can a spare parts store join the network?

Contact us at stores@4spares.com or click Join Network above. Listing your store is free and helps you reach mechanics and fleet operators nearby.

How do I track my delivery?

Track your order in real time through the 4Spares app or at 4spares.in. You get live updates from dispatch to doorstep.

What if the part I need isn't available nearby?

If no nearby store has the part, you can expand your search radius within the app to include stores at a greater distance, giving you access to a wider inventory.
